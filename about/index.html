ABOUT

Ksenia is a digital artist, currently based in Utrecht, The Netherlands. She specializes in visual art, character design and concept art. Her works mostly inspired by classical paintings, mythology, slavic folklore and macabre art.

Graduated from Moscow State Textile University named after A.N. Kosygin in 2013, majoring in costume design. Since 2014 works as independent artist.
