ABOUT

Ksenia is a Russian artist based in Utrecht. She creates digital paintings, most of which are heavily influenced and inspired by Russian literature, slavic folklore and myriads of classical painters. She posts her artworks on various online social platforms under the alias kotartist, and through her paintings with different images and metaphors Ksenia exposes her own ominous world

As an artist she loves to experiment and combine artistic style, that is close to classical paintings, with the modern digital tools, such as Photoshop and Procreate app

Graduated from Moscow State Textile University named after A.N. Kosygin in 2013, majoring in costume design. Since 2014 works as independent artist
